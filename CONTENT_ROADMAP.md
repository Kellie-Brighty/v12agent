## Content Principles

1. **Always timestamp** — "Reporting [time], [date]" format is working. Keep it.
2. **One clear milestone per post** — don't bundle multiple things together.
3. **Show, don't just tell** — terminal logs, screenshots, dashboards. Real proof.
4. **One honest emotion** — the raw reaction is what connects people.
5. **Don't skip the losses** — they're more powerful content than wins.
6. **Antigravity is a character in this story** — "me and my AI" is a great angle.
